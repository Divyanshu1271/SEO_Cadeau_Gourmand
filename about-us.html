About Cadeau Gourmand

Mission and Vision

At Cadeau Gourmand, we believe that a gift is more than just an item — it’s a story, a connection, and a celebration of life’s most meaningful moments. Founded with passion, built on quality, and driven by the joy of giving, we are proud to be Canada’s trusted name in gourmet gift baskets.

Our Story

Started in Montreal as a small family business, Cadeau Gourmand grew from handmade baskets into a nationwide brand known for thoughtful gifting. We focus on quality, personalization, and the joy of creating unforgettable experiences.

Our Mission

  • Deliver exceptional taste and artistic presentation
  • Offer personalization and branding options
  • Maintain reliable Canada-wide delivery
  • Ensure ethical sourcing and sustainability
  • Provide outstanding customer service

Our Core Values

  • Quality First: Sourced from premium artisans
  • Creativity: Every basket is a curated work of art
  • Authenticity: Human connection and transparency
  • Responsibility: Community support and eco-packaging

Meet Our Team

We’re designers, curators, food lovers, and dreamers. Each team member—from packaging to delivery—plays a role in making Cadeau Gourmand an inclusive, creative, and community-minded workplace.

Community Involvement

What Makes Us Different?
