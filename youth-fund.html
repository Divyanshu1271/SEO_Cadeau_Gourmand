Youth Fund: Empowering the Next Generation

Our Youth Funding Programs

The future belongs to the young — their energy, creativity, and ambition hold the potential to transform communities. Our Youth Fund supports and empowers youth across Canada through scholarships, grants, and mentorship programs.

What is the Youth Fund?

We help students and young entrepreneurs through financial aid, innovation funding, and professional mentorship. Every applicant deserves access to opportunity, regardless of background.

Financial Support

  • Scholarships: For high school and university students.
  • Startup Grants: For youth-led businesses and community projects.
  • Culture & Sports Sponsorships: For dancers, athletes, and artists.

Who Can Apply?

  • Age 14–25
  • Canadian residents with a goal or project
  • Students, creators, or entrepreneurs with financial need
  • We prioritize inclusion of BIPOC, LGBTQ+, newcomers, and persons with disabilities

Application Process

  1. Online form + document uploads
  2. Optional Zoom interview
  3. Decision in 2–4 weeks
  4. Ongoing mentorship and check-ins

Beyond Money: Mentorship & Support

  • One-on-one mentorship with professionals
  • Workshops in resume writing, public speaking, and financial literacy
  • Startup incubation for select grantees

Impact Stories

  • Emily: Now studying nursing thanks to a scholarship
  • Jordan: Built a mental health app used by 5,000 youth
  • Aisha: Held a community art show with 12 artists

How You Can Help

  • Donate (tax deductible)
  • Become a mentor or speaker
  • Partner with us to reach more youth

Let’s build a future where every young person has the tools and confidence to thrive.
